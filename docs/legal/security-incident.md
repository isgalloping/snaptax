# Snap1099 Security & Incident Response

**Last Updated:** June 2026

We protect your data with industry-standard encryption and monitoring. If a security incident affects your personal data, we will:

1. **Investigate promptly** and contain the issue  
2. **Notify affected users** without undue delay and, where required by law, within **72 hours** of becoming aware of a high-risk breach  
3. **Notify regulators** when required (e.g., under GDPR)  
4. **Describe** what happened, what data was involved, and steps we are taking  

## How to report a security concern

Email **snaptax.lightxforge@gmail.com** with subject line **Security Report**.

We do not request your password or full payment card details by email.
