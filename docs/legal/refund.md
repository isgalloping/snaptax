# Snap1099 Refund Policy

**Last Updated:** July 2026  
**Related:** [Terms of Service](/terms) · [Pricing](/pricing) · [Privacy Policy](/privacy)

## Overview

**Export Tax Pack** is a digital product sold **once per tax season**. Payments are processed by **Paddle**, our Merchant of Record. Paddle issues your receipt and handles payment compliance.

## Standard refunds

Refunds follow [Paddle's consumer terms](https://www.paddle.com/legal/invoiced-consumer-terms) and applicable law. Eligibility, timing, and method depend on your location and whether you have accessed the export deliverables.

## EU and UK consumers

Where applicable law provides a **14-day right of withdrawal** for digital content, you may have that right unless you expressly requested immediate delivery and acknowledged that withdrawal may be lost once export files are generated or downloaded. Contact us if you believe this applies to your purchase.

## What we do not refund automatically

- **Delete Account** in Settings removes your data but does **not** trigger an automatic Paddle refund. Request a refund separately if eligible.
- Duplicate purchases by mistake — contact us with both transaction IDs; we will help coordinate with Paddle.

## How to request a refund

Email **snaptax.lightxforge@gmail.com** with:

- Your Paddle transaction ID or receipt email
- The tax season purchased
- A brief reason for the request

Paddle may also offer refund options directly from your purchase receipt.

## Season re-export

A paid tax season includes **unlimited re-export for that season**. Needing another export file for the same season is not grounds for a refund — use **Export Again** in the app.
