# Snap1099 Pricing

**Last Updated:** July 2026

Receipt capture and AI categorization in Snap1099 are free. You pay only when you unlock **Export Tax Pack** for a tax season.

## What you pay for

**Export Tax Pack** is a one-time purchase per tax season. After payment, you can export your deductible receipts for that season as often as you need, including:

- Schedule C Mirror (PDF)
- TXF (tax software import)
- Expenses CSV
- CPA Audit Trail (ZIP)

Snap1099 is not a monthly subscription for taking photos or storing receipts on your device.

## How pricing works

- One **Paddle** checkout per tax season unlocks export for that season only.
- The price shown on this page reflects current Flag-driven pricing for the next available seat or your locked Founder tier.
- Prices may differ during the **Founder Program** (first 50 paying seats). Open the app for checkout and your exact quote.
- Paid seasons include **unlimited re-export** for that tax season.

## Founder Program (summary)

While seats remain, new buyers may lock a lower season price for future seasons if they stay active. After 50 seats or if Founder status lapses, the standard season price applies. Payment terms are in our [Terms of Service](/terms).

## Taxes

Paddle (Merchant of Record) may add VAT, sales tax, or similar charges where required by law. The total shown at checkout is final.
