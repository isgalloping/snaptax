### 4. Data Storage & International Transfers

To provide our Services, your receipt images and profile data are securely transferred to and stored on encrypted cloud servers located in the **United States**.

By using the App and agreeing to our Terms and Privacy Policy (including when you snap a receipt while online), you expressly understand and acknowledge that your data will be processed in the United States, which may have different data protection laws than your country of residence. We safeguard all international data transfers using industry-standard encryption (**TLS 1.3** and **AES-256**) and ensure our cloud infrastructure sub-processors strictly comply with the **EU-U.S. Data Privacy Framework** where applicable.
