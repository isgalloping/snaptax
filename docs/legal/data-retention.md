# Snap1099 Data Retention Policy

**Last Updated:** June 2026  
**Related:** [Privacy Policy](/privacy) · [Delete Account](/terms)

This summary describes how long Snap1099 keeps different types of data. Technical details may be updated as our infrastructure evolves; material changes will be reflected in our Privacy Policy.

## On your device

| Data | Retention |
|------|-----------|
| Receipt records (all statuses) | Up to **18 months** from receipt date; older items may be removed during background cleanup |
| Receipt photos (full resolution) | Until upload succeeds, or up to **90 days** after cloud sync, then removed locally (thumbnail may remain) |
| Encryption keys | Until you delete the app data or use **Delete Account** |

## On our servers (United States)

| Data | Retention |
|------|-----------|
| Receipt images & metadata | While your Ghost session or Google account is active |
| Account & billing records | As required for tax export entitlements and law |
| After **Delete Account** | Cloud receipts and images are permanently deleted; completion typically within **30 days** |

## Logs

| Data | Retention |
|------|-----------|
| Security and API logs (no receipt images) | Up to **90 days** on our hosting platform |

## Your controls

- **Export Tax Pack** — download your data  
- **Delete Account** (Settings) — erase local and cloud data tied to you
