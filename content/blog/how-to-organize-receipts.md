# How to Organize Receipts for Tax Season

**Category:** Expense Tracking
**Published:** 2026-06-25
**Description:** Simple habits to stop losing paper receipts and build a clean audit trail before you file.

Shoeboxes full of faded receipts are a contractor cliché. A few minutes per job keeps April calm.

## Capture immediately

Photograph every business receipt before it leaves your truck or toolbox. SnapTax works offline — sync when you have signal.

## One place for everything

Avoid splitting receipts across email, photos, and glove-compartment envelopes. One app means one export when filing.

## Match categories early

When AI suggests a category, fix it while you remember the job. Cleaner data means faster CPA review.

## Review monthly

Spend ten minutes each month scanning for personal charges mixed into business or missing meals on long job days.

## Export when ready

At tax season, unlock your Export Tax Pack once per season. Unlimited re-exports for that season are included — handy if your CPA asks for a revised file.
