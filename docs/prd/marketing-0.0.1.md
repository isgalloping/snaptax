> **Goal:** Acquire the first 50 high-quality paying users while building trust, collecting feedback, and validating product-market fit.

---

# 1. Background

SnapTax is an AI-powered tax deduction assistant designed for self-employed workers.

As an early-stage Micro SaaS, the most valuable assets are not revenue, but:

- Early paying customers
- Real usage data
- Product feedback
- Testimonials
- Word-of-mouth referrals

Instead of offering temporary discounts, SnapTax will launch a **Founder Program**.

Users are not simply buying a subscription.

They are becoming part of the first community helping shape SnapTax.

# 4. Lifetime Pricing Rules

Founder pricing remains valid only while the subscription stays active.

If a Founder cancels and later subscribes again:

- Founder pricing is forfeited.
- Current standard pricing applies.

Reason:

- Encourages long-term retention
- Prevents abuse
- Keeps Founder seats meaningful

# 13. FAQ

## Why only 50 founders?

We want to work closely with a small group of early customers who help shape SnapTax through real-world feedback.

---

## What happens after 50 founders?

Founder pricing closes permanently.

New users subscribe at the standard price.

---

## Will my price ever increase?

No.

Your founder price is locked for life while your subscription remains active.

---

## What happens if I cancel?

If your subscription is canceled, Founder pricing is permanently forfeited.

Rejoining requires paying the current standard price.

# 16. Design Principles

- Simplicity over complexity
- No countdown timers

- No misleading discounts

- Build long-term trust
- Reward early supporters

# Summary

The SnapTax Founder Program is designed to attract the first 50 paying users by offering **permanent Founder status and lifetime pricing**, rather than relying on short-term discounts. This approach creates a sense of exclusivity, encourages long-term retention, builds trust, and establishes a loyal community of early adopters who actively contribute to the product's evolution.
